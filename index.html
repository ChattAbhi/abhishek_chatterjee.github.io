About Me

I am a recent PhD graduate from the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace and Engineering at University of Texas at Arlington, where I was a part of Robotics, Biomechanics, and Dynamic Systems Laboratory and worked with Dr. Alan Bowling. My research was in the area of Contact and Impact Modeling in Rigid Multibody Dynamic Systems. Besides multibody dynamics and contact modeling, I am also interested in Robotic Manipulation (specially legged robotics and nonprehensile manipulation), Optimal Control, Path-Planning, Flexible Body Dynamics and FEM.

Before joining grad school, I compleated my bachelor's degree from Midwestern State University in Wichita Falls, Texas. My hometown is Calcutta (Kolkata), India, and I have lived in Texas, as a student, for almost 10 years (So currently I'm about 2/3 Bengali and 1/3 Texan ðŸ¤ ).
