About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace and Engineering at University of Texas at Arlington. I am a part of the Robotics, Biomechanics, and Dynamic Systems Laboratory, and my doctoral advisor is Dr. Alan Bowling. I am currently working on the modeling of contacts and impacts in rigid/deformable body systems, and my research interest includes Multibody Dynamics, Robotics and Controls. Before joining grad school, I compleated my bachelor's degree from Midwestern State University in Wichita Falls, Texas.
